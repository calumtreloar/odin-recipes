Tomato Feta Pasta

Description

This super easy dinner recipe comes straight from Tik Tok.

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Pasta of choice

Steps

  1. Place tomatoes in a baking dish, oil them up, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Nestle a block of feta cheese smack dab in the middle of the baking dish and bake at 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and chopped basil, stir to combine all that melty, cheesy goodness, and stir in cooked pasta to serve.
